Welcome To Garden Fresco - All Of Us For All Of You

Garden Fresco - A place from the heart, dedicated to unique and suitable practices

Garden Fresco looks to empower any individual from privileged and unprivileged backgrounds, who are looking to start their own farming business. We provide the necessary skills and tools required for prospects, and guide them through their journey. To us, this has nothing to do with profit, we believe that providing one with such skills is profit enough. Our services do not end with just providing the skills to start a business, we also provide farming equipment, because we understand that this can be expensive and that not everyone has the privilege to get them at their first attempt.

Garden Fresco:

  • Offer free farming programs to bring experience our youth
  • Provide shelters with high quality fresh produce
  • Provide nearby communities with high quality produce
  • Offer free consultation about anything in regard to farming
  • Live in a world of giving
