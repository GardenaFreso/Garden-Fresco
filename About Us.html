About Us

Summary

Garden Fresco is encouraged by the fathers of our founding members, who have been in the farming industry for years. They grew up with a belief that also grew in us,
the believe that giving is receiving, and since approached us with a proposal to run a new division. Motivated by this, we decided to go into the farming industry.
Farming has never been easy, we never wished for it to be, but we know that we can make something off the guidance provided by this experienced person.

Focus

Garden Fresco is a black owned agricultural based Non-profit organization that is dedicated to sustainable and unique agricultural practices.

Living In Harmony

Garden Fresco is influenced by the economy and the season, we work respectfully and compatibly with nature. Garden Fresco looks to work with profitable, high quality products to provide to anyone in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to work respectfully with nature and provide sufficient and enjoyable produce.

Our mission is to encourage the youth to go into farming by providing quality education and training.

Our mission is to reach as many lives as we can by helping those in need
