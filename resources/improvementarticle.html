What it takes to become a farmer(interview with Garden Fresco owners)

21 November
Edited by: Omphile Kolojane

"Farming is a skill that requires hard-work and determination to succeed against all odds.
You have to be able to take failure and turn it into motivation.", said the Owners of Garden Fresco.
"Starting out, we planted potatoes seedlings, not knowing that it would be a disaster. Potato require
extensive care, from the amount of water you feed them to preventing pesticides, because they are one of
food items that need to be in good condition because they are grown underground, and anything less than
the best condition can cause illnesses."
When asked what it takes to be a good farmer, the brothers mentioned the following traits...
To be a better farmer you must demonstrate a passion for:

  • Teachability
  • physical stamina
  • Knowledge about machinery
  • Learning from mistakes
  • Outdoors
